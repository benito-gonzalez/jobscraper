Show your appreciation

I guess you are wondering... "What's with this page?" - isn't the website free anymore?

Do not worry, the website is still free.

The funny thing is that we didn't consider to have such a page, but this is the result of people's request.

It seems that there are people who found jobs using our website and they would like to show their appreciation by buying us a coffee for instance (some would prefer even beer).

Unfortunately, is close to impossible to meet everyone and have a coffee, so that's why this page was born.

Long story short, nobody needs to donate (company or individual) but if you feel like, just go ahead, now you have a way to do it.

Thank you for your support and appreciation!
